ClientNetworkAdapter :
----------------------------
	1.List - List the details of all Available ClientNetworkadapters of selected LogicalPartition

	2.Create - Creates a ClientNetworkAdapter in the selected LogicalPartition[The PortVLANID is hardcoded]

	3.Returns to LogicalPartition Menu - returns to Logical Partition menu

	4.Return to ManagedSystem Menu - returns to ManagedSystem Menu

	5.Return to Main Menu -returns to Main Menu

	6.Help - shows the ClientNetworkAdapter Help content

	7.Exit - completely exits from the program
