VolumeGroup:
-------------
	VolumeGroup Menu:

	1.List - List the Details of volume group such as Physical volumes and Logical volumes

	2.Modify - shows following options,
		
		1. Add PhysicalVolume to VolumeGroup - adds a physical volume to the selected volume group(name of the disk hardcoded)  
		2.Create VirtualDisk in VolumeGroup - creates a Virtual Disk of size 1GB is Created(capacity and name hardcoded)

	3.Create - Create a Volume group with one physical volume(name of the disk hardcoded)

	4.Return to vios Menu - returns to VirtualIOServer menu

	5.Return to ManagedSystem Menu - returns to ManagedSystem

	6.Return to MainMenu - returns to MainMenu

	7.Help - Shows Help content of VolumeGroup

	8.Exit - completely exits from the Program
